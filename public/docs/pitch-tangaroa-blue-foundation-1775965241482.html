01   The Problem

What's holding you back

Marine debris data is Tangaroa Blue most powerful asset - but collecting, cleaning, and communicating it is still harder than it needs to be. Volunteers record debris data on paper at cleanups, data entry is manual, and the insights that drive policy change are locked behind spreadsheets.

Cleanup data collected on paper forms - transcription is slow and error-prone

●

No real-time visibility into debris hotspots or trends during cleanup events

●

Volunteers cannot see how their individual cleanup contributes to the bigger picture

Source-reduction campaigns need faster data pipelines to respond to emerging threats
