Downforce AU has built a corporate-channel white-glove path with NAB, Asahi, Coles, Sundown Pastoral and CEFC behind it, but it sells into large-landholder and corporate-buyer relationships. The peak-body steward running 50 to 300 hectares falls through the floor between those channels, and that is the population EMPI Stage 2 extension and outreach is trying to reach.

The architecture I have been building is software-only. A peak body operates a per-tenant digital MRV layer for its members, aggregates submission to the Clean Energy Regulator on their behalf, and the MRV operator takes no share of credit revenue. SaaS licence plus per-project issuance fee, fixed costs amortised across the cooperative. It is the same generic submission flow across soil-carbon, vegetation, blue-carbon, savanna-burning - method-specific data shapes, single platform.
