## EMPI - the program window

**Environmental Markets Participation Initiative (EMPI):** federal program funded by DCCEEW (Department of Climate Change, Energy, the Environment and Water). Co-delivered with NRM Regions Australia. Stated objective: build the capability of regional NRM bodies and First Nations groups to participate in environmental markets (carbon credits, biodiversity certificates under the Nature Repair Act, ACCU scheme).

**Stage 1 (~2023-2024):** capability and learning focus. Workshops, market literacy, baseline assessments, exploratory engagement with the Clean Energy Regulator and Department.

**Stage 2 (~2024-2026, current):** extension, outreach, project-development support. Targeted at the regional-NRM operational layer - helping regions and members move from "we understand environmental markets exist" to "we have a project pipeline with measurable participation."
