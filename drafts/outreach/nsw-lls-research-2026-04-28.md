## Organisation context

**NSW Local Land Services** is the largest single-state regional body system in Australia. 11 regions, statewide coverage, established under the Local Land Services Act 2013, working alongside the Biodiversity Conservation Act 2016. State-funded with strong landholder-services orientation: biosecurity, agricultural advisory, natural resource management, emergency management.

**NRM Regions Australia** is the federal peak for Australia's 54 regional NRM bodies. Marnie Lassen incoming CEO early May 2026 (per addendum). Tess sits on the board.

## The active program that matches our wedge

**Carbon + Biodiversity Pilot in the Riverina** (NSW LLS region):
- Part of the Australian Government's $66.1M Agriculture Biodiversity Stewardship Package
- Rewards farmers for land stewardship that stores carbon AND improves biodiversity
- Run by an LLS region under the federal program

**Biodiversity Stewardship Agreements (BSA)** under NSW BC Act 2016:
- Voluntary in-perpetuity landholder agreements
- Generate biodiversity credits
- Offset sites for development impacts
- Conservation agreements as a parallel instrument with financial incentive structure

**Implication:** NSW LLS is already operating a hybrid carbon-and-biodiversity stewardship program at sub-commercial-scale. They are the single largest test bed in AU for the wedge thesis. The dMRV gap inside their program is real and named.
