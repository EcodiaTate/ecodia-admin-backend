## The offering, one paragraph

Conservation operations platform on Capacitor + Supabase + Edge Functions, multi-tenant, six months in production at the lighthouse deployment (108 database migrations, 17 Edge Functions, $24/month server on the Sunshine Coast). Enterprise tenant tier $5,000-$7,000/mo. Software-only digital MRV add-on at $1,500/mo per active method stream, designed for sub-commercial-scale carbon and biodiversity projects that fall through the cracks of AgriProve and GreenCollar economics. Build fee covers per-tenant configuration. Platform IP held at Ecodia Labs Pty Ltd; client-facing IP attribution is Ecodia Pty Ltd (the Labs licensing chain is internal). Buyer holds a perpetual, non-transferable operating licence for their deployment, contingent on subscription. 30-day termination either side. Buyer's data is theirs, with export available on request.
