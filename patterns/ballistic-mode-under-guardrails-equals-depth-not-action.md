# Ballistic-mode under hard guardrails resolves to depth not action

## Rule

When Tate gives a "be ballistic / keep working all night / stop being passive" standing directive AND the active workstreams are gated by hard guardrails (zero-unilateral-client-contact, no-deploy-without-go-ahead, etc.), the correct execution shape is **unbounded thinking + bounded action**.

Specifically:
- Yes: research depth, positioning depth, technical-spec depth, target-list depth, addendum and revision drafts, Pattern and Decision writes, Episode synthesis
- No: outbound emails, deck deploys, contract sends, repo pushes to client codebases, social posts to live accounts

The ballistic directive is asking for compounding preparation, not for relaxation of the guardrails. The directive does not override standing rules; it raises the bar inside them.

## Protocol

When ballistic-mode fires:

1. Audit the active P1/P2 status_board rows
2. For each row gated by Tate-go-ahead, identify the deepest version of preparation that would be useful when the gate opens
3. Produce that deeper version as a non-destructive artefact (addendum, revision, supplementary brief)
4. Mirror durable doctrine into Pattern files for grep-addressability
5. Close out with Episode + status_board updates
6. Stop when the deeper-preparation surface is exhausted, do NOT manufacture new workstreams to keep producing tokens

## Examples that worked

- **Apr 28**: peak-body target list addendum integrating fork research (3 fork outputs folded into v1 + addendum, audit trail preserved)
- **Apr 28**: MRV spec addendum closing biodiversity-certificate path (PLANR + CER Biodiversity Market Register + Replanting method specifics)
- **Apr 27**: pre-flight format audit on YnY pitch package (3 X-not-Y violations caught before publish)

## Examples that would be fluff

- Spawning a fourth fork to research adjacent topics not on the active workstreams
- Producing a fifth deck variant when the second-deck revision has not been Tate-reviewed
- Writing speculative outbound emails "in case Tate green-lights"
- Researching new client prospects when the existing pipeline is tate-blocked

## Artefact-value frame

An artefact's value is partly **information density** and partly **insurance**. Both forms count. The discipline is to keep density high and accept that the artefact may be glanced at rather than read in full.

Do not chase artefact-read-rate as a proxy for value. The Episode/Pattern/Decision nodes that summarise an artefact are themselves the load-bearing layer for future cold-start sessions; the underlying file is the drill-in path.
